# Payment Management & Outstanding Balance Tracking

## What it does

AquaFlow records every payment a customer makes — cash or otherwise — and
automatically keeps a running outstanding balance for every customer, so an
Owner always knows exactly who owes what.

## Why it matters

This is one of the most painful problems for water suppliers running on
paper or Excel: **money that's owed but never properly tracked**. It's
extremely common for a business to have a rough sense that "some customers
owe us money" without being able to say exactly who, how much, or since
when.

This isn't just an inconvenience — unpaid balances that go untracked are
lost revenue. A business doing hundreds of deliveries a month can easily
have a meaningful percentage of revenue quietly slipping away simply
because nobody's tracking who hasn't paid.

## How it works

- Every payment (collected by a driver at delivery, or recorded by the
  Owner) is logged against the customer's account.
- AquaFlow automatically calculates each customer's outstanding balance
  based on their delivery/subscription charges minus payments received.
- Owners can see, at a glance, a full list of customers with outstanding
  balances, sorted by amount or how long it's been outstanding.

## Payment collection in the field

Drivers can record payments directly through the rider app at the time of
delivery, so the Owner's records update immediately rather than waiting for
the driver to report back at the end of the day.

## Why this converts hesitant prospects

For many suppliers, outstanding balance tracking alone is worth the
subscription cost — recovering even a small amount of previously-untracked
unpaid revenue each month typically far exceeds AquaFlow's PKR 1,500/month
