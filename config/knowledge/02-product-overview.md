# Product Overview

## What AquaFlow does

AquaFlow is a complete water delivery management system covering every part
of running a water supply business:

| Area | What it replaces |
|---|---|
| Customer Management | Paper customer lists, scattered phone numbers |
| Delivery Management | Paper delivery registers, memory, WhatsApp reminders |
| Driver Management | Verbal delivery instructions, no accountability |
| Bottle Tracking | Guessing how many bottles are with each customer |
| Monthly Subscriptions | Manually tracking who's on a recurring plan |
| Payment Management | Cash-in-hand with no record |
| Outstanding Balance Tracking | "I think they still owe us something" |
| Analytics Dashboard | No visibility into the business at all |
| Rider Mobile App | Drivers calling in for instructions |
| Customer Portal | Customers calling to ask about their account |
| Invoice Printing | Handwritten receipts, or nothing at all |
| Receipt Printing | Handwritten receipts, or nothing at all |

## Roles in AquaFlow

AquaFlow has three roles, each seeing only what's relevant to them:

- **Owner** — full control of the business: customers, deliveries, drivers,
  bottles, subscriptions, payments, and the analytics dashboard.
- **Delivery Boy (Rider)** — sees their own delivery route for the day,
  marks deliveries complete, tracks bottles given/collected, and records
  payments received, all from the rider mobile app.
- **Customer** — has their own portal to see their delivery history,
  outstanding balance, and account details, without needing to call and ask.

## How it fits into a daily routine

A typical day with AquaFlow:

1. The Owner sees today's scheduled deliveries on the dashboard, generated
   automatically from active subscriptions and one-off orders.
2. Deliveries are assigned to drivers.
3. Each driver opens the rider app, sees their route, and marks off
   deliveries as they complete them — including bottles delivered/collected
   and any payment taken.
4. The Owner sees delivery status update in real time, and can see who paid,
   who didn't, and who still owes money.
5. Customers can check their own delivery and payment history any time
   through the customer portal, instead of calling to ask.
6. At the end of the day/week/month, the Owner checks the analytics
   dashboard to see how the business is actually performing.

## What AquaFlow is not

AquaFlow is not a generic inventory or logistics platform — it's built
specifically around the workflow of a water delivery business: recurring
customers, bottle deposits/returns, and daily or scheduled delivery routes.
We don't claim to support unrelated industries or workflows outside this.
