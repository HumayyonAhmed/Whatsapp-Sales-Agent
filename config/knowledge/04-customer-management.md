# Customer Management

## What it does

AquaFlow gives every water supplier a single, searchable record of every
customer they serve — replacing scattered paper lists, memory, and phone
contacts.

Each customer record includes:

- Name and contact number
- Delivery address (residential or commercial)
- Delivery preferences (e.g. how many bottles, which days)
- Subscription status (active monthly plan or one-off orders)
- Full delivery history
- Full payment history and current outstanding balance
- Notes (e.g. gate codes, preferred delivery time, special instructions)

## Why it matters

Water suppliers doing 20+ deliveries a day are often serving hundreds of
recurring customers. Without a proper system:

- New staff have no way to know a customer's preferences or history
- A customer who calls to ask "did I pay last month?" can't get an answer
  without someone digging through paper
- There's no way to spot which customers are the most valuable, or which
  ones have gone quiet and might be lost to a competitor

## Adding and managing customers

Owners can add new customers directly, or a customer can be added the first
time a delivery is scheduled for them. Once added, a customer's full history
is tracked automatically going forward — no manual re-entry needed for
repeat deliveries.

## Commercial vs residential customers

AquaFlow supports both individual/residential customers and commercial
accounts (shops, restaurants, offices) that may have different delivery
volumes, schedules, or billing arrangements.

## Customer self-service

Customers with portal access (see `11-mobile-app.md`) can view their own
delivery history and outstanding balance without needing to call the
supplier directly — reducing the number of "did I pay?" calls owners get.
