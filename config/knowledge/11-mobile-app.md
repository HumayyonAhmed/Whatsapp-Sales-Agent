# Rider Mobile App & Customer Portal

## Rider Mobile App

The rider app is what delivery staff (the Delivery Boy role) use in the
field. It's designed to be extremely simple — far simpler than the paper
process it replaces — so staff can learn it quickly with minimal training.

What a driver can do in the rider app:

- See their assigned deliveries/route for the day
- Mark each delivery as completed
- Record bottles delivered and bottles collected (empties)
- Record any payment collected from the customer

Everything a driver does in the app updates the Owner's dashboard in real
time — no need to call in or report back at the end of the day.

**Access:** the rider app works through the browser as part of AquaFlow's
Progressive Web App — no app store download is required.

## Customer Portal

The customer portal gives a supplier's own customers self-service access to
their account:

- View delivery history
- View current outstanding balance
- View payment history

This reduces the number of "did I pay last month?" or "when's my next
delivery?" calls an Owner or their staff has to personally handle, and gives
customers a more professional, modern experience than a purely informal
WhatsApp-based relationship.

## Why this matters

Both of these exist to solve the same underlying problem: **manual work and
miscommunication**. Every question a driver would otherwise have to call in
to ask, and every question a customer would otherwise have to call to ask,
is now answered directly by the system itself.
