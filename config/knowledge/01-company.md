# Company Overview

## Who is AquaFlow

AquaFlow is a water delivery management software company built specifically
for water suppliers across Pakistan. We help water suppliers stop running
their business out of paper registers, Excel sheets, and WhatsApp groups,
and give them one simple system to manage customers, deliveries, drivers,
bottles, subscriptions, and payments.

## Who we serve

Our ideal customer is a water supplier doing **20 or more deliveries per
day**. This includes:

- Bottled water (19L) delivery businesses
- Water filtration plant owners who deliver to homes and offices
- Suppliers serving a mix of residential and commercial (office, restaurant,
  shop) customers
- Suppliers running multiple delivery riders/drivers

Smaller suppliers (a handful of deliveries a week) can still use AquaFlow,
but the system is built and priced around the needs of an operation that
has outgrown paper and Excel.

## Why we exist

Every water supplier we've spoken to in Pakistan runs into the same set of
problems as they grow past a certain size:

- Deliveries get forgotten because there's no system tracking who's due today
- Customer records get lost, damaged, or are simply illegible
- Nobody can say for certain how many bottles are out with which customers
- Outstanding balances pile up because nobody's tracking who still owes money
- The owner spends hours every week on manual bookkeeping instead of growing
  the business
- There's no way to see basic numbers — revenue, top customers, delivery
  performance — without doing it all by hand

AquaFlow was built to solve exactly these problems, for exactly this kind of
business.

## What AquaFlow is, technically

AquaFlow is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — it runs in a normal web
browser on desktop or mobile, with no app store download required for the
main system (a dedicated rider app is available for delivery staff). It has
temporary offline capability for continuing to work during short internet
outages, and syncs automatically with our backend (Supabase) once internet
is available again.

## Our promise to customers

We only tell customers what AquaFlow actually does. We don't invent
features, promise custom development, or promise things that aren't built
yet. If a customer asks something we're not sure about, we say so honestly
and have a team member follow up with the real answer — that's what makes
us trustworthy, not a chatbot that says yes to everything.
