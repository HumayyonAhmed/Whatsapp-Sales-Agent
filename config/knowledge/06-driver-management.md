# Driver Management

## What it does

AquaFlow lets an Owner manage every delivery driver/rider as a proper part
of the system, not just a name on a whiteboard.

For each driver, AquaFlow tracks:

- Their assigned deliveries for the day/route
- Delivery completion status in real time
- Bottles they're carrying/have delivered/collected
- Payments they've collected on behalf of the business

## Why it matters

Without a system, driver accountability depends entirely on trust and
memory. Owners doing 20+ deliveries a day with multiple drivers have no way
to answer basic questions like:

- Did this driver actually complete their route today?
- How many bottles does this driver currently have with them?
- How much cash has this driver collected today that hasn't been handed in?

AquaFlow answers all of these automatically, because every delivery, bottle
movement, and payment is recorded by the driver themselves through the
rider app at the time it happens.

## The Delivery Boy role

Delivery staff get their own simple view (the rider app — see
`11-mobile-app.md`) showing only what's relevant to them: their route for
the day, and the ability to mark deliveries complete, record bottles, and
log payments. They don't see the Owner's full business data — just their
own job for the day.

## Accountability without micromanagement

Because everything is logged automatically as drivers do their job, Owners
get accountability and visibility without having to personally check in
with each driver throughout the day.
