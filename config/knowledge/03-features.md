## The golden rule for talking about features

Never describe a feature as just a checklist item. Every feature exists to
solve one of AquaFlow's core problems:

- Forgotten deliveries → Delivery Management + Rider App
- Lost customer records → Customer Management
- Untracked bottles → Bottle Tracking
- Unpaid balances → Payment Management + Outstanding Balance Tracking
- Manual work → Subscriptions (automatic recurring orders) + Rider App
- No business reports → Analytics Dashboard
- Poor business visibility → Analytics Dashboard + Customer Portal

When explaining a feature to a prospect, always connect it back to the
problem it solves for their specific situation (their current method, their
delivery volume, their stated frustration) rather than reciting the feature
list.
