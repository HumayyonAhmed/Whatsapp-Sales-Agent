# Delivery Management

## What it does

AquaFlow schedules, tracks, and confirms every delivery — generated
automatically from active subscriptions, plus support for one-off orders.

For each delivery, AquaFlow tracks:

- Which customer, address, and how many bottles
- Which driver it's assigned to
- Scheduled date/time
- Status: pending, in progress, completed, or missed
- Bottles delivered vs bottles collected (empties)
- Payment collected at the time of delivery, if any

## Why it matters

The single biggest complaint we hear from water suppliers on paper or Excel
is: **forgotten deliveries**. When a business is relying on a driver's
memory or a page in a register, it's easy for a customer to get missed —
and an unhappy customer is a customer who starts looking at competitors.

With AquaFlow:

- Today's delivery list is generated automatically — nobody has to remember
  who's due
- Drivers see their exact route on the rider app, so nothing is missed
- Owners can see in real time which deliveries are done and which aren't
- If a delivery is missed, it's visible immediately, not discovered days
  later when a customer complains

## One-off vs recurring deliveries

AquaFlow supports both:

- **Recurring deliveries** generated automatically from a customer's active
  monthly subscription (see `08-subscriptions.md`)
- **One-off orders** for customers who order occasionally rather than on a
  fixed schedule

## Delivery confirmation

Drivers confirm each delivery from the rider app at the point of delivery,
including bottles given/collected and payment received. This means the
Owner's dashboard reflects what's actually happening in the field, in real
time, rather than end-of-day guesswork.
