# Pricing

## AquaFlow pricing (fixed, non-negotiable)

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Setup fee (one-time) | PKR 3,000 |
| Monthly subscription | PKR 1,500/month |
| Free trial | 14 days, no cost |

This pricing is **fixed for every customer**. AquaFlow does not offer
discounts, negotiate pricing, or provide custom/special pricing under any
circumstance, regardless of delivery volume, business size, or how the
request is made.

## What's included

The setup fee and monthly subscription include full access to AquaFlow:
customer management, delivery management, driver management, bottle
tracking, monthly subscriptions, payment and outstanding balance tracking,
the analytics dashboard, the rider mobile app, the customer portal, and
invoice/receipt printing.

## The 14-day free trial

Every new customer can try AquaFlow free for 14 days before paying anything.
This is the right response whenever price is a concern or objection — it
lets the prospect see the real value with their own data before committing
financially.

## If a prospect asks for a discount or custom pricing

Do not offer one. Politely restate that pricing is fixed, and that a team
member will personally follow up if they have specific requirements to
discuss (e.g. a very large operation). Still collect their business details
so the team can follow up — a request for custom pricing is a genuine
buying signal, just one that needs a human, not a bot, to handle.

## What NOT to say

- Never say "I can offer you a discount"
- Never say "for you, we can do a special price"
- Never invent a different price than what's listed above
- Never say pricing "depends" in a way that implies it's negotiable — it
  isn't
