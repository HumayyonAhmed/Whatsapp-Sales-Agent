# Frequently Asked Questions

## How much does AquaFlow cost?
PKR 3,000 one-time setup fee, plus PKR 1,500/month. A 14-day free trial is
available before any payment is required. Pricing is fixed and not
negotiable.

## Is there a free trial?
Yes — 14 days, full access, no cost.

## What kind of businesses is AquaFlow for?
Water suppliers across Pakistan, ideally doing 20 or more deliveries a day —
bottled water delivery businesses, filtration plant owners delivering to
homes/offices, and similar operations.

## What devices does it work on?
Any device with a modern web browser — desktop or mobile. It's a
Progressive Web App, so no app store download is required for the main
system. Drivers use a dedicated rider app.

## Does it work without internet?
It has temporary offline capability for short outages, but needs internet
periodically to sync data with the server. It's not designed for extended
fully-offline use.

## Can my drivers use it?
Yes — each driver gets their own rider app access showing their assigned
deliveries for the day.

## Can my customers see their own account?
Yes, through the customer portal — delivery history, payment history, and
outstanding balance.

## Does it track how many bottles are with each customer?
Yes — bottle tracking automatically maintains a running balance per
customer based on deliveries and collections (empties returned).

## Does it track who owes me money?
Yes — Payment Management and Outstanding Balance Tracking automatically
calculate what each customer owes based on their deliveries/subscription
charges minus payments received.

## Can I see reports on my business?
Yes — the Analytics Dashboard shows deliveries, revenue, outstanding
balances, top customers, and driver performance in real time.

## Can I print invoices and receipts?
Yes, both are supported directly from the system.

## How do I get started?
Reply here to book a Free Live Demo or start the 14-Day Free Trial, and a
member of the AquaFlow team will personally follow up with you.

## Do you offer discounts for larger operations?
No — pricing is fixed for every customer. If you have specific
requirements, a team member can follow up personally to discuss.

## Can I cancel anytime?
It's a monthly subscription; a team member will confirm the exact
cancellation process when they follow up — we won't guess at specifics
here.
