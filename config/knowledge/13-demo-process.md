# Demo Process

## The two options

Once a lead is qualified or shows real interest, offer them a choice
between:

1. **Free Live Demo** — a walkthrough of AquaFlow with a real team member,
   tailored to their business.
2. **14-Day Free Trial** — hands-on access to try AquaFlow themselves with
   their own data, free for 14 days.

Either is a good outcome. Some prospects prefer to see it explained first
(demo); others prefer to just try it themselves (trial). Let them choose.

## What happens after they agree

This is important: **the AI does not book anything automatically.**

When a visitor agrees to a demo or trial:
1. Confirm warmly and thank them for their interest.
2. Let them know a member of the AquaFlow team will personally reach out to
   them (on this same WhatsApp conversation, or by phone) to confirm timing
   and get them set up.
3. Never say or imply the demo is "booked", "scheduled", or "confirmed" —
   only that the team will be in touch to arrange it.

This keeps expectations accurate: a human always closes the loop personally,
which also gives the sales team a chance to build a real relationship with
the prospect.

## Why this two-step process matters

Water suppliers are often busy, hands-on business owners. A personal
follow-up from a real team member — rather than an automated calendar
booking — tends to convert much better in this market, and lets the sales
team tailor the demo to what the prospect actually cares about (their
specific pain point, delivery volume, current method).

## What a good demo covers

A demo (once handled by a human team member) typically walks through:

- Setting up customers and subscriptions
- The daily delivery flow from the Owner's dashboard
- The rider app from a driver's point of view
- Bottle tracking and outstanding balance tracking in action
- The analytics dashboard

The AI's job is only to get the prospect to the point of agreeing to see
this — not to run the demo itself.

## Do options (The two options - Roman Urdu)

Jab lead interest show kare toh unhe 2 options dein:

1. **Free Live Demo** — Humari team ka member online call par unhe AquaFlow ka pura system unke business ke mutabiq samjhaye ga.
2. **14-Day Free Trial** — 14 din ke liye system khud use kar ke dekhin apne data ke sath.

Dono hi ache outcomes hain. Kuch log pehle samajhna chahte hain (demo) aur kuch khud chala ke check karna chahte hain (trial). Unhe khud choose karne dein.

## Agree karne ke baad kya hota hai (What happens after they agree - Roman Urdu)

Bohat important: AI khud se demo ya trial book nahi karta.

Jab customer demo ya trial ke liye maan jaye:
1. Unhe thanks bole aur confirm karein.
2. Batayein ke AquaFlow ki team ka member unse WhatsApp ya phone call par rabta karega taake time set kiya ja sake aur account setup ho sake.
3. Kabhi yeh mat kahein ke demo "book", "schedule" ya "confirm" ho gaya hai — bas yeh kahein ke team rabta karegi set karne ke liye.

Is se customer ki expectation sahi rehti hai ke koi human unse contact karega.

## Yeh two-step process kyun zaroori hai (Why this two-step process matters - Roman Urdu)

Pani ka business chalane wale log kafi busy hote hain. Automatic calendar booking ke bajaye when a team member reaches out personally (jab koi team member khud rabta karta hai) toh conversion behter hoti hai aur team customer ke problems aur delivery volume ke mutabiq demo adjust kar sakti hai.

## Demo mein kya dekhaya jata hai (What a good demo covers - Roman Urdu)

Demo mein humari team yeh cheezein cover karti hai:
- Customers aur subscription set up karna.
- Owner dashboard se daily delivery flow.
- Driver mobile app ka demo.
- Bottle tracking aur payments tracking.
- Analytics dashboard.

AI ka kaam sirf demo ke liye customer ko raazi karna hai, demo khud chalana nahi hai.
